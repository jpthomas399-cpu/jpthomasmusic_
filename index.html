About

Rooted in the church,
refined on the stage.

JP Thomas is a Denver-born vocalist whose musical journey began in the black church and evolved through gospel, R&B, and chorale music, ultimately leading him to discover his passion for vocal arranging. Now a senior at Berklee College of Music, he has arranged vocals for over 20 performances in the past four years, consistently delivering spiritually and emotionally powerful experiences.

JP's versatile artistry and arranging expertise make him a standout presence in any performance he's part of — bridging sacred tradition with contemporary craft, and bringing a rare emotional intelligence to every arrangement.

What I Care About Right Now +

Right now I'm very curious about the collaboration of gospel music in secular genres and vice versa. I'm also exploring how to further my arranging abilities and make them more adaptable to other styles outside of R&B, Gospel, and Pop. Something else I'm also concerned about is how independent artists can build sustainable lives without having to change who they are for the music industry or even making themselves profitable. If you care about these things as well, my journey is worth watching.
